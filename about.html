About Brew House

Our story, mission, and the people behind your favorite cup of coffee

Where It All Began

Brew House was founded in 2005 by coffee enthusiast Michael Chen, who had a simple vision: create a space where quality coffee and community could thrive together.

What started as a single storefront in San Francisco's Mission District has grown into dozens of locations across the country, but our commitment to handcrafted coffee and personal connections remains unchanged.

Every Brew House coffee shop is designed to be a welcoming "third place" between work and homeâ€”a comfortable environment where ideas are born, relationships are built, and exceptional coffee is served.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to transform the coffee experience through quality, sustainability, and community. We believe that great coffee can create meaningful moments and connections.

Quality First

We source only the highest-quality beans and train our baristas to craft the perfect cup every time. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every sip delivers an exceptional experience.

Community Focus

We create warm, inclusive spaces that bring people together and support local initiatives. Our cafes serve as gathering places where relationships are built and ideas are shared.

Sustainability

We're committed to ethical sourcing and environmentally responsible practices across our business. From farm to cup, we ensure our impact on the planet is positive and sustainable.

Innovation

We continuously explore new coffee varieties, brewing methods, and customer experiences. Our passion for innovation drives us to push boundaries and create unique coffee moments.

From Bean to Cup

Our coffee journey begins with carefully selected beans from sustainable farms around the world, creating direct trade relationships with growers in regions known for exceptional coffee.

We roast in small batches at our own facilities, allowing our coffee masters to bring out the unique character of each bean. The result is coffee with distinct personality, reflecting its origin and our careful craftsmanship.

Every Brew House barista undergoes extensive training to ensure that the final cup served to you represents the peak of coffee perfection.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Brew House, sustainability isn't just a buzzwordâ€”it's a core value that guides every decision we make. We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint while maximizing our positive impact on coffee-growing communities.

Ethical Sourcing

We pay premium prices for our coffee, ensuring farmers earn a living wage and can invest in sustainable practices. Our direct trade relationships support long-term partnerships with coffee-growing communities.

Waste Reduction

We're working toward zero waste operations through composting, recycling, and innovative packaging solutions. Our comprehensive waste management program helps minimize our environmental impact.

Community Support

Our Brew House Foundation invests in healthcare and education in coffee-growing regions around the world. We believe in giving back to the communities that make our business possible.

Carbon Neutral

We're on track to become carbon neutral by 2025, with investments in renewable energy and carbon offsets. Our commitment to reducing emissions extends across our entire supply chain.
